3. New Jersey Home Improvement Contractor Requirements

3.1 Licensing

Tillerstead LLC is registered as a Home Improvement Contractor with the New Jersey Division of Consumer Affairs (License #13VH10808800). Our license status can be verified at the NJ License Verification website.

3.2 Written Contracts Required

Under the New Jersey Contractors' Registration Act (N.J.S.A. 56:8-136 et seq.) and the Consumer Fraud Act, all home improvement contracts over $500 must be in writing. Written contracts will include:

  • Complete description of work to be performed
  • Total contract price and payment schedule
  • Estimated start and completion dates
  • Contractor's legal name, address, and HIC registration number
  • Notice of the homeowner's right to cancel
  • Any warranties or guarantees provided

3.3 Right to Cancel (Three-Day Rescission)

Important Consumer Protection Notice:

For home improvement contracts entered into at your residence, you have the right to cancel the contract without penalty or obligation within three (3) business days after receiving a signed copy of the contract, pursuant to N.J.S.A. 56:8-136.

To cancel, you must notify Tillerstead LLC in writing within this period. Upon cancellation, any payments made will be refunded within 30 days.

3.4 Deposit and Payment Terms
