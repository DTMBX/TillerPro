Flexible Payment Solutions for Lasting Tile & Bath Projects

At Tillerstead, we believe every project deserves uncompromising quality—regardless of budget. Our flexible payment options and expert guidance ensure you never have to sacrifice durability or craftsmanship.

Every project begins with a detailed written scope and a transparent plan. Our installation methods strictly follow TCNA standards and manufacturer guidelines, so you can trust your investment will stand the test of time.

Payment Options Tailored to You

  • Pay in Full: Perfect for smaller projects or when you want the fastest turnaround.
  • Milestone-Based Payments: Start with a deposit, then pay as each phase is completed and verified—so you’re always in control.
  • Phased Project Approach: Focus on what matters most now (like waterproofing and showers), with the option to complete additional work later—without compromising quality.
  • Third-Party Financing: Instantly check offers from trusted lenders via Acorn Finance. No obligation, no impact on your credit to pre-qualify.

How Our Process Works

  1. Consult & Plan: We visit your site, take precise measurements, and develop a written scope. If phasing is best, we’ll map out each step together.
  2. Transparent Proposal: Receive a clear, line-item estimate with defined scope, schedule, and terms—so there are no surprises.
  3. Select Payment Option: Choose the payment structure that fits your needs, or check third-party financing offers online in minutes.

Financing is always optional. We never cut corners or skip critical prep to "make the numbers work." If needed, we’ll phase your project—so you get lasting results, not costly shortcuts.
